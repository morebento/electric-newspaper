Adelaide, South Australia  ·  February – July 1998
Australia's first independent online daily newspaper

The Electric Newspaper was Adelaide's first independent online daily newspaper, published from February to July 1998. Founded at the Virtual Artists web studio on Rundle Street by veteran journalist Hendrik Gout, and hosted by early ISP Internode, it ran news, essays, arts, film, books, food, sport, and letters — 711 articles across seven months, written by working journalists and edited to professional standard. No print edition was ever produced. This site is a complete archive, restored in 2024 from the original Unix server tar file.
